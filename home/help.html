Help & Instructions

Welcome to the Quiz Game!

Here are some instructions to get you started:

How to Play

  • Select a quiz type from the main menu.
  • Answer the quiz questions to the best of your knowledge.
  • Submit your answers and see your score.
  • Try to beat your high score!

Scoring

Your score is based on the number of correct answers you provide. The more correct answers, the higher your score.

Timer

Some quizzes may have a timer. You need to answer the questions within the given time limit.

Good Luck!

Have fun and enjoy playing the quiz game. Test your knowledge and challenge your friends!
